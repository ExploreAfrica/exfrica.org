About

Our knowledge about the country we live in, visit or plan to live in is scattered around the world in books, journals, databases, websites, encyclopedias, and in the minds of people. Now, imagine if this information is gathered, presented and made readily available to everyone – anywhere – at a moment’s notice. This dream is becoming a reality through Explore Africa's Project.

We’re building a global community dedicated to exploring and opening the underserved local African knowledge through the collaborative support of country citizens working together to keep the African knowledge alive, summarized and accessible. We also believe this collection of vast Information on an open platform about African countries should be created by same country citizens who know it best.

When complete, Explore Africa will provide a free access to trusted, and comprehensive information about every country in Africa.

Researchers

The platform will be a place for researchers to understand and learn everything about Africa.

Schools

The platform will be used by teachers, learners, and education experts to share, reuse, and remix African local Knowledge.

Citizens

The platform will provide an avenue for citizens to learn facts and share things about their country with Africans and the world at large.

Tourists

The platform will provide immediate information with tourist interest, which promotes the commercial sector and generate value to citizens.

A global access to knowledge about every country in Africa. Our transparent community-based processes will promote participation, accountability and trust. When you join Explore Africa you'll become part of a global community aimed at building a brighter Africa

Our products

Explore Africa is a 'federated' umbrella for grassroots open knowledge initiatives in Africa.

Explore Nigeria

A mobile application that takes you on an educational tour through the Giant of Africa. There by helping students, parents, teachers and tourist to know about Nigerian's History, Culture, Tradition, Festivals, Recourses, opportunities and much more... .

Explore The Gambia

A mobile application that takes you on an educational tour through the Smiling Coast of Africa. There by helping students, parents, teachers and tourist to know about Gambians History, Culture, Tradition, Festivals, Recourses, opportunities and much more...

Africa is not a Country

The continent is made up of over one billion people living in 54 nations and alot to offer with wide variation in economic and government systems with further differentiation in language, religion, and shared history.

How can i contribute to the Explore Africa: Get Involved

As a Developers: All content is built in a simple semantic HTML5 format rich with built-in accessibility features to ensure it can be read by everyone. While we embrace open technologies, developers can build on and use the platform.
As an Author: All content produced is available under a Creative Commons open-content licenses making it easy for authors to share their work - allowing others to use and reuse.
As a Desginer: You can help tell a story via visuals, communication culture, and their visual nature helps overcome literacy and language barriers.
As a partner:We are mindful that no single organisation or initiative is never going to solve all the problems, we are therefore open for partnership
